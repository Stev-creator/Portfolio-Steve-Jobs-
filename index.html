About Steve Jobs

Steve Jobs was a visionary entrepreneur, inventor, and designer who co-founded Apple Inc. and Pixar Animation Studios.

He is widely recognized as one of the most innovative and successful business leaders of the last century.

Apple I

The first personal computer designed and hand-built by Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak.

Macintosh

The first commercially successful personal computer to use a graphical user interface.

iPhone

A revolutionary mobile phone that combined a touchscreen interface with a mobile operating system.
